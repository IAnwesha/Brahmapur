About Berhampur

Brahmapur is a city on the eastern coastline of Odisha, India. Brahmapur is also known as the Silk City due to the famous Silk Patta Sarees. The city is known for its love for the theatre and cinema. Infact the Ganjam Kala Parishad and Prakasam Hall are one of the oldest theatre halls in the country.

Popular Attractions

Gopalpur Beach

Located on the east coast of the Bay of Bengal, the Gopalpur Beach is a luxurious beach located in Odisha. It is popular for being one of the few sites in India where Olive Ridley Turtles nest.

Tara Tarini Temple

Tara Tarini Mandir is a Hindu shrine of Adi Shakti in the Kumari hills on the bank of Rushikulya river near Purushottampur in Ganjam district of Odisha, around 28km from Brahmapur city.
